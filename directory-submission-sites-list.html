What is Directory submission

Directory submission is an off-page SEO technique where you submit your website's URL to online directories that categorize websites based on their content, niche, location, and other parameters. The objective of directory submission is to improve the visibility and online presence of your website by creating backlinks from authoritative directories.

When you submit your website's URL to directories, you provide details like the website's title, description, keywords, and other relevant information to help the directory's editor categorize your website correctly. Once your website is approved and listed in the directory, it creates a backlink to your website, which can help improve your website's search engine ranking.

Directory submission used to be a popular SEO strategy in the past, but its effectiveness has reduced over time as search engines have become more sophisticated in their algorithms. Nowadays, submitting to high-quality directories can still be beneficial as part of a broader SEO strategy, but it should not be the only tactic used for SEO.
